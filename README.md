## ğŸš€ How to Use

1.  Visit the [website](https://deadpixeltester.github.io/).
2.  Select the appropriate test for your device (**Monitor Test** or **Mobile Test**).
3.  The screen will turn a solid color (starting with Black). Inspect your screen for any dots that remain unlit (dead pixels) or are stuck on a different color.
4.  To change the color, **click anywhere on the screen**, use the **Next** button, or press the **Spacebar** on your keyboard.
5.  To exit the test, click the **Exit** button or press **ESC**.
